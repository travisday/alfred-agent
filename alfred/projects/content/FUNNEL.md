# CONTENT FUNNEL

How the platforms connect and what each one does.

---

## THE 3 BUCKETS

### 1. Instagram — Top of Funnel (Awareness & Interest)

**Audience:** Cold — they don't know you yet
**Content:** Short reels (talking head + b-roll), 25-30 seconds, one concept per reel
**Goal:** Stop the scroll → shift a belief → earn the follow
**Conversion target:** Follower → free Substack subscriber
**Volume:** 7 talking heads/week + 21 b-roll/week

**What IG does:**
- Introduces the idea that anyone can build apps with AI
- Removes barriers and busts myths
- Establishes Travis as a credible, relatable guide
- Creates curiosity that can't be satisfied in 30 seconds

**What IG doesn't do:**
- Teach how to actually build anything
- Go deep on any topic
- Sell paid content directly

**Bridge to Substack:** CTAs like "I break this down step by step in my newsletter — link in bio"

---

### 2. Free Substack — Mid Funnel (Trust & Depth)

**Audience:** Warm — they followed from IG or found the newsletter, opted in with their email
**Content:** 3 free posts/week going deeper on topics than IG allows
**Goal:** Build trust → demonstrate expertise → make paid content feel like a no-brainer
**Conversion target:** Free subscriber → paid subscriber
**Volume:** 3 free posts/week

**What free Substack does:**
- Goes deeper on topics IG introduces at surface level
- Gives enough value that they trust your paid content is worth it
- Teases paid guides without being salesy — shows the gap between "knowing about it" and "knowing how to do it"
- Builds a direct relationship (email > algorithm)

**What free Substack doesn't do:**
- Give full step-by-step tutorials (that's paid)
- Repeat the same surface-level takes from IG
- Try to convert cold audiences (they come pre-warmed from IG)

**Bridge to paid:** Every free post should leave them thinking "I get WHY/WHAT, now I need the HOW" — with a natural pointer to paid content

---

### 3. Paid Substack — Bottom of Funnel (How-To & Community)

**Audience:** Hot — they've consumed enough free content to trust you and want real guidance
**Content:** 1 detailed paid guide/month + community access
**Goal:** Deliver real results → retain subscribers → create advocates
**Conversion target:** Retention + word of mouth
**Volume:** 1-2 paid post/month

**What paid Substack includes:**
- Full step-by-step guides on building apps with AI
- The actual "how" that free content points to but doesn't fully deliver
- Group chat access (ongoing community)
- Monthly group call with Travis

**What makes it worth paying for:**
- Actionable, complete tutorials they can follow and get results from
- Direct access to Travis (chat + calls)
- Community of people doing the same thing
- Saves them weeks of figuring it out alone

## THE CONVERSION PATH

```
Cold audience (doesn't know you)
    │
    ▼
IG Reel (30s belief shift)
    │
    ▼
IG Follow (wants more)
    │
    ▼
Free Substack (deeper trust, email captured)
    │
    ▼
Paid Substack (full guides + community)
```

**Key principle:** Each stage should make the next stage feel obvious, not forced. IG makes them want depth. Free Substack makes them want the full playbook. Paid delivers the playbook + access.

## WHAT GOES WHERE

| Topic | IG | Free Substack | Paid Substack |
|-------|-----|---------------|---------------|
| "You don't need to code" | 30s myth bust | Why this is true + what you need instead | Full guide: your first app without coding |
| Tool recommendations | "These are the only 2 tools you need" | How each tool fits the workflow + tradeoffs | Step-by-step setup and first project walkthrough |
| Career leverage | "This is how I doubled my comp" | What skills employers actually want now | Guide: building a portfolio that gets interviews |
| MVP building | "Investors fund MVPs, not pitch decks" | What makes a good MVP + planning framework | Full tutorial: idea to deployed MVP |
| Personal automations | "This saves me 10+ hours/week" | 3 automations worth building + why | Build-along: create your first automation |

## METRICS THAT MATTER

| Platform | Primary Metric | Why |
|----------|---------------|-----|
| IG | Saves + Shares | Shows content resonates beyond vanity views |
| Free Substack | Open rate + click-through to paid | Shows trust and conversion potential |
| Paid Substack | Retention rate | Shows you're delivering real value |
