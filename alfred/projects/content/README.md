# Content Project

A comprehensive content marketing strategy across Instagram, free Substack, and paid Substack designed to convert cold audiences into paying community members.

## Overview

This project defines a three-tier content funnel that transforms Instagram followers into paying Substack subscribers through progressive trust-building. The strategy is built on the principle that each platform has a specific role and audience stage.

## The Three-Bucket Funnel

```
Cold Audience (doesn't know you)
    ↓
Instagram Reels (30s belief shift)
    ↓
Free Substack (deeper trust + email)
    ↓
Paid Substack (full guides + community)
```

### 1. Instagram — Top of Funnel
- **Audience:** Cold (don't know you yet)
- **Goal:** Stop the scroll → shift a belief → earn the follow
- **Volume:** 7 talking heads/week + 21 b-roll/week
- **Content:** 25-30 second reels, one concept per reel

### 2. Free Substack — Mid Funnel
- **Audience:** Warm (followed from IG, opted in with email)
- **Goal:** Build trust → demonstrate expertise → make paid feel inevitable
- **Volume:** 3 free posts/week
- **Content:** Deeper exploration of topics teased on IG

### 3. Paid Substack — Bottom of Funnel
- **Audience:** Hot (consumed enough to trust + want guidance)
- **Goal:** Deliver real results → retain → create advocates
- **Volume:** 1-2 detailed guides/month
- **Content:** Complete step-by-step tutorials + community access

## Key Metrics

| Platform | Metric | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|

| Free Substack | Open rate + CTR to paid | Indicates trust and conversion potential |
