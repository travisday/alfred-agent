# Where to Start With Your App Idea

**Type:** T6 — First Step
**Day:** Saturday

## Hook

"This is the first thing you should do if you have an app idea — and no, it's not learning to code or hiring a developer."

## Topic

Step one is not coding, not hiring — it's opening ChatGPT and describing your idea. Planning first removes the biggest blocker.

## Talking Points

- Credential: "My name is Travis. I'm a software engineer."
- Open ChatGPT. That's the first step.
- Describe your app idea like you're telling a friend. "I want an app that helps me track my workouts" or "I need something to manage client appointments."
- Ask ChatGPT to help you plan what features it needs. What screens. What data. What the user flow looks like.
- That's step one. Not coding. Not hiring. Not watching 40 hours of tutorials. Just planning.

## Outcome Tie

"Because this one action removes the 'I don't know where to start' blocker that stops most entrepreneurs from ever building their idea."
