# Learn Skills, Not Specific Tools

**Type:** T4 — Myth Busting
**Day:** Thursday

## Hook

"This is the one skill that will matter most for building apps in the future — and no, it's not mastering a specific platform."

## Topic

Platforms like Lovable, Replit, and Bolt will change or die. The transferable skill is learning how to work with AI coding agents.

## Talking Points

- Credential: "My name is Travis. I'm a software engineer."
- People think they need to master a specific platform like Lovable, Replit, or Bolt. That's the wrong approach.
- These platforms will change, get acquired, or shut down. The tool you master today might not exist in a year.
- What matters is the skill underneath: how to describe what you want clearly, how to give context, how to debug, how to test and deploy.
- That's working with AI coding agents. That's the transferable skill. It works regardless of which specific tool you use.

## Outcome Tie

"Because the tool you learn today might be obsolete in a year, but knowing how to work with AI to build software will matter for decades."

## CTA

"Follow me to learn the skills that don't expire."
